Purpose
 
Someone has a role and they need a ranked shortlist of candidates + supporting evidence on the candidates/ranking.
 
Intended Users
 
ELT (Enterprise? Leadership Team)
HRLT (HR Leadership Team)
May have interest in the talent health of a specific discipline.
Have a role/disc. In mind and want to understand "organizational pipeline health" (do we have a lot of people ready for x role or not?) of a certain job discipline
Will probably want to filter to specific org/leadership chain. May want to know the relative comparison between the org and the top candidates. Maybe the top in this filtered org is actually number 20,000 overall for the role.
Talent Managers
I want to understand our candidates for some hypothetical role. The req may not exist.
Hiring Managers
Have a req, find candidates
 
 
Who are our candidates?
 
Looking for "ready-now/releasable talent pool of executives"
 
Seeking a "pool" approach to talent that could be ready. Need to understand development needed to get these candidates all the way there.
So does this mean we want candidates that are almost there, but maybe need a little bit of training?
 
Use case EX:
Example: Currently searching for an internal slate of candidates for a Chief of Staff role with x, y, z criteria.  We need a tool to source candidates enterprise-wide for consideration.  This would show where we have ready and releasable talent and potential capability gaps.

User Flow
 
User Inputs one of the following:
A req. number if they have one
A role description + job title
 
We will show the description, desired skills, required skills from their input.
 
Desired/required skills will be extracted from the input initially, and will be editable by the user to add/remove any in the list.
 
 
Features
 
The MVP will have basic features to what we can do. The next/later features are a wish list of things that we could do and how that would bring value to the intended users/company.
 
MVP
(Think of features for hiring managers)
Search via req
Search via role desc. + skill inputs
Skill extraction on role info
Skills extracted can be edited (add/remove)
Candidate job history won't always have req data. We will only be searching/comparing by the job title at the start
 
 
Requirements
Deterministic candidate results
Scoring algorithm to rank a candidate for a search
Skill Evidence
Input: List of required skills for a req, List of desired skills for a req, coefficient weight
Output: Skill similarity score - A normalized (with the other pillars) score that represents the similarity between the input role's skills and the skills of each employee/candidate.
How
Combine required skill evidence score with desired skill evidence score
Role History Evidence
Input: Job title of input role, coefficient weight
Output: Role history similarity score - A normalized role history score that represents the similarity between the input title and each employee's historical and current job titles. Weighted more towards recent roles.
Experience Evidence
Atlas professional summary: who you are, what you generally do summarized
(*) Year-end closeouts: what you've done, achievements, projects you've worked on.
Input: Job title, job description, coefficient weight
Output: Experience similarity score - A normalized score that represents the similarity between the input title + description and each employee's professional summary & recent Atlas closeouts. Weighted more towards recent roles.
